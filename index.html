Learn how to pronounce classical Latin

Repetition helps with learning, but one of the challenges with learning classical Latin is hearing it spoken. It is difficult to understand word stress and the pronunciation of diphthongs, vowels, and consonants using written pronunciation guides alone since you're never really sure if what you're vocalizing is accurate.

Perhaps this can help with that. Of course, resources like Google Translate exist that have a Latin option, but the pronunciations are often inaccurate as far as classical Latin is concerned. The following pronunciations of selected classical Latin words are taken from Wheelock's Latin, the classic introductory course based on ancient authors. Happy learning!
